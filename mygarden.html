How to setup My Garden feature

Step 1. Download Arduino IDE and these files
Step 2. Plug included DHT11 and NodeMCU into a power source
Step 3. Open files with Arduino IDE and change the ssid and password to your WIFI credentials
Step 4. Click the upload button in the top right
Step 5. Open the serial monitor and copy and paste the IP address into your browser
Step 6. All done! If you would like to access this information remotely, use remote desktop connections such as Google Remote Desktop
